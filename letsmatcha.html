LET'S MATCHA

about the project

"Let's Matcha" is a digital infographic on the subject of Food and Drink. It illustrates how to make a matcha latte in just 5 simple steps. Green, tasty, quick, and easily prepared, matcha latte is a famous green tea drink from Japan that is well-known all over the world and popular in Vietnam. The target audience of this project is young peopled aged 20 and up who loves matcha. It will not only help matcha lovers to enjoy their own homemade matcha latte but also save them money.
