ĐÀN CA TÍCH

about the project

"đàn ca tích" is an traditional music festival project that blends traditional Vietnamese music with a modern aesthetics. The website's visually striking design, featuring a harmonious color palette that capture the tradition and a unique logo, seamlessly bridges the gap between old and new cultural elements. The user-friendly interface ensures easy navigation, allowing visitors to explore detailed sections and buying tickets.
