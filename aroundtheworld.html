AROUND THE WORLD

about the project

The "Around the World" project focuses on creating sets of vector icons inspired by architectural landmarks worldwide, with a strong influence from the artistic style of Van Gogh. These icons are designed using strokes and thin charcoal brush, creating a unique aesthetic reminiscent of Van Gogh's paintings. The project ensures a visually compelling representation of landmarks, balancing aesthetics with practicality. The adaptability of these icons to different sizes guarantees readability and functionality, making them versatile and suitable for various applications.
