HUES OF HOME

about the project

"Hues of Home" is a web-based interactive poetry project that combines poetry with an engaging digital experience. The project's objective is to create a captivating experience by integrating the art of poetry with custom design, animations, and interactivity through the coding languages HTML and CSS. Inspired by the childrenâ€™s poetry book entitled "The Yard Corner and Sky Interval" by Tran Dang Khoa, the project draws from the poem "Buoi Sang Nha Em" to depict the peaceful landscapes of the Vietnamese countryside. Through this, it evokes a deep sense of nostalgia and connection to Vietnamese rural life serves as the foundation for the project's visual style and themes.
