Vi Nguyen
Multi-disciplinary Designer

hi. i'm Thuy Vi, a graphic designer and illustrator based in Vietnam, currently studying at RMIT University. My main focus is on making illustrations, especially for children's books, using cool techniques like grain and texture.

i'm inspired by the small, beautiful things around me. I love exploring nature and capturing its charm in my designs. In the moments away from the design desk, you'll find me playing tennis, painting, or taking care of my little garden. Who needs a social life when you have paintbrushes and plant friends, right?

thanks for stopping by, and feel free to check out my portfolio to see my creative side in action!
