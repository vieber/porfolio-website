COLOR

about the project

“Color” is a short film on the topic of little things in life that aims at young people that live in the sped-up lives. The short film is about the story of a young boy living in the black-and-white world with no interest in life. Because of the fast-paced life, he ignores the little moments and forgets about the worth of life. “Color” is created to remind young people to slow down and appreciate all the little things around them. Setting aside time in the day for small pleasures is important because it relieves stress and anxiety, provides space for contemplation, and gives you a break from work responsibilities.
